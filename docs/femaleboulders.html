As of this writing, there appear to be 47 climbs rated V13 (8B), 12 climbs rated V14 (8B+), and 4 climbs rated V15 (8C) that have had female ascents.

The most climbed V13 boulder is Fragile Steps with 17 ascents; followed by Nuthin but Sunshine and Automator, The with 7 and 6 ascents, respectively.

The most climbed V14 boulder is New Base Line with 6 ascents; followed by Jade and Golden Shadow with 3 and 2 ascents, respectively.

V15 Club

Currently it seems as though four women have climbed a V15 boulder: Ashima Shirashi with two (!) - Sleepy Rave and Horizon; Kaddi Lehman with Kriptos; Mishka Ishi with Byaku-dou; and Oriane Bertone with Satan i helvete bas.

Who Is the Strongest Female Boulderer Ever?

The following are two bar plots based on the data in the above list showing who has sent the most hard boulder problems and who has put up the most hard boulder first ascents (FAs).

The strongest climber by most hard boulder sends is Alex Puccio with 47 ascents; 12 more than the Isabelle Faus with the second most ascents. However, Isabelle Faus has the most FAs with 6; 5 more than Alex Puccio, and an impressive amount of hard sends to her name. Finally, as stated above, Ashima Shirashi has climbed two V15s, along with Kaddi Lehman, Mishka Ishi, and Oriane Bertone who have climbed one each - something that neither Alex Puccio nor Isabelle Faus has done.

Trends Over Time

Starting in 2002 with an ascent of Travesia de Balzola in Spain by Josune Bereziartu, the number of women to have climbed V13 or higher has consistently gone up, especially in 2014. I must have my number wrong because something crazy seems to have happened in 2020. Although with the pandemic and the olympics, it’s hard to say.

How Many Women Can Climb V13 or Higher?

At the moment, there are 47 women in the world that have sent V13. After which there is a steep drop off, with 12 having sent V14, and 4 having sent V15.

Which Countries Have the Hardest Boulder Problems for Women?

The Top Countries with the hardest female sends are the United States, South Africa, Switzerland, and France.

What About Age?

What is the age of strong female climbers today?

The mean, median and mode age of strong female climbers today is 28, 29, and 24, respectively.

The youngest V13 female climber today is 16 years old. The oldest V13 female climber today is 50 years old.

What about stats on how old they are when they send the route?

The mean, median and mode age of strong female climbers when they sent is 24, 25, and 25, respectively.

The youngest age to have climbed V13 or higher is 10 years old. The oldest age to have climbed V13 or higher is 35 years old.
